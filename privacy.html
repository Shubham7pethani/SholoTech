Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

At SholoTech ("we", "us", "our"), your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have — across our website and the apps we publish (such as Image to File Converter, Status Saver, and others).

1. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little as possible. Depending on how you interact with us, this may include:

  • Contact details you provide — name and email when you book a call or get in touch.
  • Project details you share with us voluntarily during consultations.
  • Basic usage data — anonymous analytics like pages visited and device type, to improve the site.
  • App permissions — our apps only request permissions needed to function (e.g. storage access for file conversion), and process data on your device wherever possible.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and schedule consultations.
  • To deliver, maintain, and improve our services and apps.
  • To communicate important updates about a project you've engaged us for.

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-party services

We use trusted third-party tools that may process limited data on our behalf, including scheduling (Cal.com), video calls (Google Meet), and calendar/email (Google). These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

4. Data retention & security

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described here, then delete it. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us. We'll respond as soon as we reasonably can.

6. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
